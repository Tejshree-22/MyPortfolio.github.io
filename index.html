    Testing Philosophy

    My focus is on creating scalable, maintainable automation frameworks that reduce manual effort while increasing test reliability and coverage.

    I prioritize risk-based testing, ensuring critical business flows are always validated, and leverage automation strategically where it delivers maximum value.

    Ultimately, my goal is to deliver high-quality, stable, and user-centric software through a balance of automation, exploratory testing, and continuous improvement.
